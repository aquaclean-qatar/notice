FaCade Cleaning Services

Our Façade Cleaning Services stand as an effective and efficient solution to your needs. In providing excellence, we utilize 99.9% Pure Water systems to provide stain-free glass cleaning solutions. We increase weather resistance while lowering possibilities of corrosion, bringing forth a positive image while increasing the value of your property.

The advantage of using rope access methods mainly lies in the safety and speed with which workers can get to or from difficult locations and then carry out their work, often with minimal impact on other operations and the nearby area. Another major benefit is that the combination of the total man-hours and the level of risk for a particular task (man-at-risk hours) is often reduced when compared with other means of access and their associated risks and costs.

We always follow IRATA standards and make in-depth risk assessments before we start. To ensure extra safety, all our rope access teams comprise Rope access technicians and one supervisor to plan and oversee the operation.

The advantage of the Pole Access System is that it doesn’t limit window cleaners to just cleaning windows. It can be used to clean virtually anything, from roofs to commercial signage and cladding. Most importantly, it reduces the risk of injury and damage to property, while only using pure water to clean windows.
